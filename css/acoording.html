Bootcamp Courses

Designed for beginners and students without any IT experience, the IT Fundamentals course covers the essentials for a career in information technology. This course is ideal for non-technical professionals and empowers students to use features and functions of common operating systems, establish network connectivity, identify common software applications, and more.

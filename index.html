BOOTCAMP FEATURES

  • Duration Immersive and standard bootcamps are 16-28 weeks long. Self-paced bootcamps are 6-9 months long.
  • Mentor-led Sessions Immersive bootcamps have 1 mentor session twice a week, standard bootcamps have 1 mentor session once a week, and self-paced bootcamps have five on-demand mentoring sessions.
  • Capstone projects Capstone projects will demonstrate the skills and knowledge you learned throughout bootcamp.
  • Job Placement Services Our job placement services will help you with resume building, mock interviews, and internship/job opportunities.
  • Hands-on labs Hands-on labs sharpen your skills and prepare you for real world scenarios.
  • Annual course access With any learning track, students have 12 month access to 100+ courses.
